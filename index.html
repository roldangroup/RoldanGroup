About Our Company

Founded in 2008, Roldan Group Services Corp has grown from a small local builder to one of the region's most trusted construction firms. Our mission is to deliver exceptional construction services through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and client-focused solutions.

We pride ourselves on our experienced team, stringent quality control measures, and commitment to safety and sustainability in all our projects, big or small.
